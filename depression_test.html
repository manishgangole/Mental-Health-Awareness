Depression Test Online

Depression is a complex and multifaceted experience that can impact various aspects of life. Depression can manifest in different ways, affecting mood, energy levels, and overall daily functioning. The causes of depression too are often interwoven. Hopefully, with the help of this tool, these different symptoms and factors can be easier to detect and be aware of. It's important to note that this questionnaire is not a diagnostic tool, but rather a means to gain insights into certain feelings and behaviors associated with depression. Connecting with yourself is far more important than one label. The results are confidential and your honest responses will contribute to a better understanding of your own emotional experiences.
